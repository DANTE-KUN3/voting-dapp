Dante

Dante is the main protagonist of the Devil May Cry series. He is a demon hunter and son of the legendary demon Sparda. Dante is known for his cocky attitude, stylish combat skills, and his iconic dual pistols Ebony and Ivory.

Kyrie is a supporting character in Devil May Cry 4 and serves as a love interest for Nero. She is a kind-hearted and caring individual who often finds herself caught in the middle of demonic conflicts.

Nero

Nero is a protagonist introduced in Devil May Cry 4. He is a young demon hunter and possesses the Devil Bringer, a demonic arm with incredible strength.
